You Are Not Alone

Depression is treatable, and suicidal thoughts can be overwhelming — but help is available right now. Reaching out is a sign of strength. Many young people recover and go on to live full lives with the right support.

If You Are in Crisis or Having Suicidal Thoughts

Call or text 9-8-8 immediately — Canada's national Suicide Crisis Helpline. It is free, confidential, 24/7, and available in English and French.

📞 Text or Call: 9-8-8

Trained responders are there to listen without judgment and help you through the moment.

Other Steps You Can Take

  • Talk to a trusted adult, teacher, or school counselor
  • See your family doctor or a mental health professional
  • Visit your nearest hospital emergency department if you feel unsafe
  • In immediate danger, call 911

If you are thinking about suicide, please reach out — help is available 24/7. You matter.
